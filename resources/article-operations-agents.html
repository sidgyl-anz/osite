From chatbot pilots to operational agents

AI pilots fail when they stop at chat. Operations need context, governance, and a control layer.

Why pilots stall

Chatbots lack operational context, permissions, and evaluation. Without ontology, AI cannot reliably map real-world entities.

The control layer

Ontology AI introduces a governed object model, agent builder, and workflow orchestration to execute real actions safely.

What to do next

Start with a high-impact workflow, model the ontology, and set evaluation metrics before scaling.
